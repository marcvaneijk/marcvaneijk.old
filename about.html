With a strong background on fabric components and vital management features, Marc embraced the Cloud Platform from day one. He works with many Service Providers and Enterprise Organizations, extending their datacenters into hybrid clouds with Microsoft Azure and Azure Pack. His experiences from real life deployments are shared by speaking at regional and international events. Marc has an active role in the Dutch Cloud Platform community. He blogs for hyper-v.nu that has profiled itself around the Cloud Platform and is well-known for in-depth blogs on fabric components and Azure Pack.
